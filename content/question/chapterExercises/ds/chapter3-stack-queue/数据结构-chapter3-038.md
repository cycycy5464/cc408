A. Q.rear==(Q.front+1)8MaxSize
B. (  Q.rear+1)号MaxSize==Q.front+1
C. (Q.  rear+1)      号MaxSize==    Q.front
D. Q.rear==Q.front

[tag_link]

正确答案：D
